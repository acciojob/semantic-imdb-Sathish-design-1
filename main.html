Lagaan

Actors | Quotes

Lagaan is a sports drama film set in 1893 in a small village in Champaner, Gujarat. The villagers face a harsh drought and are challenged by the British to a game of cricket to waive off their lagaan (tax).

Actors

Aamir Khan, Gracy Singh, Rachel Shelly were some of the main actors.

Quote

"We will play for our freedom." - Bhuvan
